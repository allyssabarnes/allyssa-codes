Look for content like this:

```html
<div class="digest-post-embed" data-attrs="..."/>
```

The div isn't closed properly.

Classes to look for:
- `.digest-post-embed`
- `.poll-embed`
- `.image-gallery-embed`

>[!tip] Tip: you can search posts for a class and it will list all the posts that contain that class.
